To create the final videos:

1.- With VLC Media Player record screen, save as MP4 (check details of how to do so)

Then to Trim video in duration and frame size:

2.- With Windows Video Editor trim duration to the desired one, delete sectins of the video we don't want to show.
3.- Take new video to PowerPoint to redice frame size, use presentation mode to do so. The sections which are out 
of the presentation screen are not shown in screen

4.- Use VLC to record again screen.
